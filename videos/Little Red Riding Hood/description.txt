Title: Little Red Riding Hood

Description:
Join Little Red Riding Hood on a magical adventure through the forest as she learns the importance of staying on the path. This classic fairytale features a cunning wolf, a brave woodcutter, and a heartwarming lesson about listening to family. Perfect for bedtime or circle time, this beautifully narrated story is designed to spark imagination in children of all ages.
